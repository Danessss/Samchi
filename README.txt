SAMCHI Business Website 2023
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You can use index.html file for the Web in Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Opera and Safari.

No external libraries or frameworks needed.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
How to use?

-Open the index.html and navigate through the whole page. 

-For easier navigation, you can click the buttons on the navbar or top right corner 
and it will jump to the section that was clicked.

-On the about tab, you can see the information and history of the business.

-Scroll down to the map section and you will see the exact pinned location via google map.
you can click the map for better view, more information about the store's location, and
even directions from you current location.

-On the last section or the Contact Us section, you can see all of the information to
contact the management like the Address, Phone Number, and Email. For convinient contact,
you can directly input your queries on the contact form and it will automatically sent
to my email address for the meantime.
