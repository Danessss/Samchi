    • About Us

    Welcome to SAMCHI Korean Mart, your one-stop destination for all things Korean! We are a premier grocery store dedicated to bringing you the authentic flavors and delights of Korea. At SAMCHI Korean Mart, we take pride in offering an extensive range of high-quality Korean products, carefully curated to cater to your every need. From the iconic kimchi to the mouthwatering instant ramen, from crispy chips to delightful candies, and even indulgent ice cream, we have it all. Our shelves are filled with a wide variety of Korean food and snacks that will transport you to the vibrant streets of Seoul.

    History

    Established by the visionary Miram Family in April 2021, SAMCHI Korean Mart was born out of a passion to share the rich culture and exquisite taste of Korea with our valued customers. What started as a small venture in Sta. Inez, Bacolor has now blossomed into a thriving business that caters to the growing demand for Korean products in the local community.
